How to use this word generator

Pick your game and a difficulty, tap the button, and you get a random word or phrase to act out, draw, or describe — no word lists to print, no repeats to worry about. One person looks at the word while everyone else guesses. It's the fastest way to start Pictionary, Charades, or Catchphrase with no setup.

Which game is which?

  • Pictionary — you draw the word (no letters, no numbers, no talking) and your team guesses before the timer runs out.
  • Charades — you act it out silently. Great for actions, activities, and movies.
  • Catchphrase — you describe it with words (but never say the word itself) and race to get your team to shout it.

Picking the right difficulty

Easy words are simple, concrete, and kid-friendly — perfect for young players and warm-ups. Medium mixes in short phrases and trickier objects. Hard throws in abstract ideas, movie titles, and multi-word phrases that make great pros sweat. Mix teams? Let each player choose their own difficulty.

Quick rules and tips

  • Use a 60-second timer per turn and one point per correct guess.
  • Generate a batch of ten so the next player always has a word ready.
  • No spelling, no mouthing the word, and no pointing at real objects in the room.
